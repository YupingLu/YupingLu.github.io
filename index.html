Research Interests

I am interested in graph algorithms and tools for biological data. Recently, I worked on clustering algorithm robustness. We compared different genome-scale clustering algorithms and we gave the definition of robustness as comparison metrics for these algorithms. According to our experiments, hierarchical clustering and paraclique have higher robustness than the others. I also worked on a biclique enumeration R package. You can download it here. And I am also interested in machine learning and deeping learning.
